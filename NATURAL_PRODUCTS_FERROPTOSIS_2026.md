## 3. FSP1-Targeting Natural Products

### 3.1 Current Status

**Direct FSP1 inhibitors from natural products:** NOT YET REPORTED

Known FSP1 inhibitors are synthetic:
- **iFSP1**: First FSP1 inhibitor (Doll et al.)
- **FSEN1**: Substrate pocket binder (PDB 9M3M)
- **viFSP1**: NADH pocket binder (species-independent)
- **icFSP1**: 3-phenylquinazolinone class, condensate former

### 3.2 Why No Natural Products?

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| FSP1 discovered recently | 2019-2020 |
| NADH/CoQ pocket is unique | Not similar to known enzyme pockets |
| Screening focus on synthetic | Large pharma focused on synthetics |
| Natural product coverage gap | Not screened systematically |
