## IBD Biology

### Inflammatory Bowel Disease
| Aspect | Crohn's Disease | Ulcerative Colitis |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Location** | Any GI tract | Colon + rectum |
| **Pattern** | Skip lesions | Continuous |
| **Inflammation** | Transmural | Mucosa only |
| **Surgery** | Often required | Colectomy possible |

### Key Pathways
| Pathway | Genes | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| **Autophagy** | ATG16L1, IRGM, NOD2 | Bacterial clearance |
| **IL-23/Th17** | IL23R, STAT3, JAK2 | Immune response |
| **Epithelial barrier** | ECM1, HNF4A, PTPN2 | Gut integrity |
| **Immune regulation** | TNF, IL10, CARD9 | Inflammation |
